82KW on Grid SPV plant Dhoot Arcade , Gurgaon

GENERATION PER DAY : 328 UNITS PER DAY

SAVING PER YEAR : : Rs 838,040/-

TECHNOLOGIES USED : Tier 1 Solar Panels , Grid Tie Solar Inverter , AC & DC CABLES

  • Design Type : Tile type placement on Flat Roof tops The Modules are placed on the ground level horizontally and suitable number of modules is connected in series. The structure frame does not have a leg assembly.
  • We integrate and recommend only the Tier I Solar Panels which use the ‘A’ grade cells with cell efficiency above 20%. The manufacturers proposed by us are global companies with installed capacity of gigawatts worldwide and have all certifications to prove their efficiency and warranty for 30 years. We choose Solar panels after looking at the Credibility of the manufacturer
  • Solar Panel Structure Assembly (‘Rails’) are drilled along with points where existing sheet is drilled with columns. Rails take the weight of Solar Panels by itself and are fixed by using chemical anchoring to prevent water leakage .
  • Inverter being heart of the solar plant we propose to integrate the best German technology inverters. Grid connected PV systems are designed to operate in parallel with and interconnected with the electric utility grid. Good quality inverters can even run for 25 years.
  • We integrate all the small and large equipment’s required for the commissioning of the Solar Power Plant. All equipment’s including cablings and the installation methodology are followed as per the installation guidelines and manuals by the Panel & Inverter manufactures
